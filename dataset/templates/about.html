About PlantAI Pro

PlantAI Pro is a cutting-edge AI-powered platform designed to help farmers and researchers quickly detect diseases in plant leaves by analyzing images.

Our system uses convolutional neural networks (CNNs) trained on a large dataset of leaf images to classify diseases with high accuracy. For transparency and trust, Grad-CAM visualizations highlight the parts of the image influencing the modelâ€™s decision.

This project is aimed at improving plant health management and boosting crop yields worldwide by leveraging the power of AI and computer vision.

Dataset & Model

We trained our models on a publicly available dataset with over 50,000 images covering multiple plant species and diseases. The models are regularly updated and validated to ensure accuracy and reliability.
